About Us

We are a group of space-walking enthusiasts. We believe that walking in space is truly the exercise of the future. It's definitely more entertaining and exhilirating than running a marathon in Boston.

Let's Go For A Walk IN SPACE!!!

The Earth was small, light blue, and so touchingly alone. We need more people up in space to walk around it. There's plenty of room for everyone! So I formed SPACEWALKERS, the solar system's premiere group of space-walking enthusiasts!

Space-Walking is open to everyone from around the world. Eveyone is welcome to join us. We even have space litter clean up parties. NB: You must bring your own spacesuit and provide your own transportation.
